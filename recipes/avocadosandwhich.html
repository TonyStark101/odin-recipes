Avocado Sandwich

The Avocado Sandwich is a classic meal! Very delicious and simple to make! You will wonder how you ever lived without it! It is extraordinarily based and redpilled.

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado (or a bunch of guacamole)
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Vegetables (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 tablespoon of Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast your bread
  2. Spread the avocado onto the bread
  3. Add your salt and pepper
  4. Place your vegetables into the sandwich (optional)
